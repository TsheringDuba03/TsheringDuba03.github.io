My Food Review

My Food Review

Design Details

  • Category: Mobile Application
  • Description: This application inform the users about the restaurants, their menus and allows the users to read the restaurant’s reviews and ratings as well as provide one.

Project Overview

The Product

My Food Review App to let customers know the foods menu as well as read restaurant’s reviews provided by the customers. Since many people have a hard time finding a quality restaurant our app comes into rescue for such situations.

The Duration

Start : August 2022

End : November 2022


The Problem

People want to enjoy quality food but it’s difficult to find restaurants that serve good food

The Goal

Designing a app that that allows the people to view the reviews and ratings of restaurant nearby .

My Role


My role is UX researcher and UX designer

My Responsibility

Conducting user research.
creating wireframes, prototyping and mockups.
Conducting usability study.

User Research Summary

I conducted research and created emphathy map to understand th users that I am designing the app for. I’ve also included those users that are mostly left out. . After reviewing the users needs and wants, I meticulously designed the wireframe and prototype which is user friendly and accessible to all . The primary group of people identified during research were working people barely gets time to cook for themselves and they are busy working and barely gets time to cook and eat so they prefer to eat out at restaurants that server quality food.

Personas

Tashi is a medical nurse and a foodie who barely gets time to cook for himself and

User Journey Map


Ideation

Drawing paper wireframe of each screen of the app on paper ensured that the elements that made it to digital wireframes would be well-suited to address user pain points. I prioritized a quick and easy access to restaurants, their menus and the ratings and the review of the restaurants to help users save time


Digital Wireframes

After I finished the paper wireframe. I started designing these wireframes digitally to get a clear picture so I used a wire framing tool called the Figma to create the digital wireframes and as per the initial design phase, I made sure the restaurants reviews and ratings were easily accessible.


Low Fidelity Prototype

After completing the digital wireframe, I started of with creating low fidelity
